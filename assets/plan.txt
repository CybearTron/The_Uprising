Genre: Side Scroller
Rule: Take one, leave the rest
Setting: Military
Theme: Rich versus the poor

Idea:
Action side-scroller where you can have only one weapon.
You can save only one person out of 3 and leave the rest to die.
You have to survive along with the other person against the enemy troops.
The Meerkats have attacked you and now its upon you to save your companion.
Also you can't be damaged by the enemy bullets but your companion can. The companion might die due to your own bullets too!!
